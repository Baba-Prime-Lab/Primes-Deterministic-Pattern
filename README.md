<p>A comprehensive research framework unveiling the structural laws governing prime numbers through the dual study of Prime Patterns and Gap Patterns.</p>

<h2>Research Scope</h2>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Prime Distribution Patterns:</strong> 10 fundamental structural properties.</li>
  <li><strong>Prime Gaps Theory:</strong> 7 distinct properties governing gap distribution.</li>
  <li><strong>Prime Exploration:</strong> Advanced methodologies for identifying prime sequences.</li>
  <li><strong>Precision Analytics (Pi(x)):</strong> Implementation of the BaBa-X and BaBa-LnX pathways for Zero Statistical Error estimation up to 10^17.</li>
</ol>
